Quartz Clothing

About Us

Quartz Clothing is a brand new, up and coming designer clothes brand aimed at creating a safer world to cycle in.

Our History

We were founded through the Mind Your Head competition, a project to develop products to prevent traumatic brain injuries or aid those who suffer from them. Seven students from a world-leading college came together with one idea: we wanted to save lives.

Our Products

We sell apparel embedded with our unique glow-in-the-dark design. In the day it looks like a trendy fashion, at night, you're lit up on the road to increase your visibility whilst maintaining your cool. We stock a range of tshirts and jumpers in many sizes and fits. Click here to have a peek.

Who We Are

We are a group of seven from UTC Cambridge, a specialist science college in the East of England,: Jimi, Louis, Jade, Leah, Daniel & Ayoub.
